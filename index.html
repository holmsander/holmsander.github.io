Context based frequency dictionary for Mandarin

"Beijing university of forign language" scraped 15 billion characters of essays from different language context. This data was however clunky with many duplicates and bugs due to the nature of the written chinese language. I patched these bugs in the data using over 30 python scripts, some for cleaning, some for verifying cleaning. Then I made cleaned data publicly available at freqchinese.com. Due to the way chinese words are constructed of characters there are also a lot of interesting relationships between words, these are also available via the normal search function. This was mainly a project of building a fast backend (PostgreSQL) but still capable of hundreds of thousans of entries with relationships.
