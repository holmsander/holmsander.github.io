2024

Context-based Mandarin frequency dictionary

I used a 15B-character essay corpus scraped by Beijing Foreign Studies University, which contained many duplicates and errors due to the nature of written Chinese. I cleaned and validated the data with 30+ Python scripts, then published the refined dataset at freqchinese.com. The dictionary also captures relationships between words based on shared characters, accessible through search. The project focused on building a fast PostgreSQL backend capable of handling hundreds of thousands of interconnected entries. What I learnt building this: Creating SQL tables with relationships and medium complex search functions designed to make back-end and front-end communicate very fast.

2025

Fokos, productivity focused social media app

This app turns time tracking into a shared, motivating experience. Start a custom Pomodoro session or run a simple timer to log focused work. After each session, you can add a short description and optional photo to capture what you accomplished. Your sessions are saved in a personal logbook and shared with friends, creating a social feed of real productivity. Seeing others stay focused helps motivate you to do the same—while the time-based nature of each post encourages meaningful work instead of passive scrolling. Built with TypeScript and React, with Supabase powering the backend. This app worked in closed environment, only tested with few users. What I learnt: building larger projects with ai, how to make ai with limited memory understand large contexts
